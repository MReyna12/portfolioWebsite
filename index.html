About Me

Hello, my name is Michael Reyna and I am an attorney-turned-software developer. I was a deal lawyer whose practice focused on commercial real estate, corporate, and finance law.

After four years of practicing law, I decided to make the jump into the world of software development and I have not looked back ever since.

I am a self-taught software developer, with experience in JavaScript, CSS, and HTML. Thanks to my previous career, I am able to adapt to a variety of challenges and projects, whether familiar or unfamiliar, while setting and managing client expectations.

When I am not coding, I enjoy playing basketball, video games, working out, and spending time with my wife and dog.
